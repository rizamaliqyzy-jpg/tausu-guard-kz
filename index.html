The Problem

Kazakhstan's mountains
are beautiful — and dangerous

Ile-Alatau, Shymbulak, Charyn Canyon — millions of visitors every year. But when something goes wrong, rescue teams are blind. No tracking. No alerts. No system.

🌑

Caught after dark

Hikers underestimate sunset times and trail difficulty. At high altitude, temperatures drop 18°C after dark. Most accidents happen in the final hours of a hike.

73%
of incidents occur after 16:00
📵

No signal, no help

Cell coverage ends above 2,000m on most Kazakhstani trails. Once a hiker is in trouble, there is no way to call for help or share their location with anyone.

61%
of rescue calls involve solo hikers
🩺

Unknown medical info

When an injured hiker is found unconscious, rescue teams have no access to blood type, allergies, or emergency contacts. Every minute of delay costs lives.

2×
higher survival with pre-shared medical data
🗺️

No centralized system

Park rangers, rescue teams, and families have no shared view of who is on the mountain. There is no platform in Kazakhstan purpose-built for outdoor safety tracking.

0
dedicated outdoor safety platforms in KZ
How It Works

Three steps to a safer hike

TauSu Guard works seamlessly — for the hiker, and for the people responsible for their safety.

1
📋

Register before you go

Hikers register on the website or app. They fill out their medical profile — age, blood type, allergies, and emergency contact — before starting their hike.

Takes under 2 minutes. Saved securely. Accessible to rescue teams instantly.
2
📍

Share your location

With one tap, hikers enable GPS location sharing. Their position is shown live on the admin map. They select their trail and check in at each checkpoint.

Works on phone GPS. Satellite integration coming for zero-signal zones.
3
🛡️

AI monitors, alerts fire

TauSu Guard watches every hiker's pace, position, and the weather. If someone stops moving or bad weather hits — alerts fire automatically.

Sunset countdowns, weather warnings, and SOS alerts to emergency contacts.
Features

Everything a safety platform needs

Built specifically for Kazakhstan's outdoor environment — mountains, lakes, and the people who explore them.

🗺️

Live trail map

Detailed maps of Ile-Alatau, Shymbulak and other trails. Every registered hiker shown as a live pin with their trail, elevation, and status.

⏱️

Sunset countdown

Real-time countdown to sunset for each active hiking zone. AI-generated descent alerts based on the hiker's current position and trail difficulty.

🌦️

Weather alerts

Live weather data for each trail. Automatic warnings pushed to hikers when rain, wind, or temperature drops are forecast during their session.

🩺

Medical profiles

Every registered hiker's blood type, allergies, and emergency contacts stored securely. One click for rescue teams to access full medical info on the admin dashboard.

🔔

Smart alerts

No movement for 30+ minutes? Behind checkpoint schedule? Out of safe zone? Automatic alerts fire to the admin dashboard, the hiker, and their emergency contact.

📡

Satellite GPS

Satellite-based tracking for zero-signal mountain zones via Garmin inReach and LoRa mesh networks.

Coming soon
🏊

Swimmer safety zones

Kapchagai, Burabay and other lake zones mapped with safe and danger boundaries. Waterproof wristband chips track swimmers in real time.

Coming soon
👥

Group hiking

Register as a group with a designated group leader. The admin dashboard tracks the whole group together, flags if anyone splits from the group's route.

Coming soon
🌐

3 languages

Full support for English, Russian, and Kazakh — making the platform accessible to all hikers across Kazakhstan and the region.
